The Celebration

✦   A day full of joy   ✦

24 May · Prior Evening
Ponnazhaipu
The beloved bride is ceremonially escorted from her ancestral home to the mandapam — accompanied by nadaswaram, family, and the joyful blessings of all who gather to send her forth.
25 May · Morning
Muhurtham
The sacred wedding rites — where two souls are bound together under the watchful eyes of the divine, elders, and all who hold them dear.
25 May · Following Ceremony
Reception
A grand celebration in true style — vibrant music, traditional attire, and an abundant feast of authentic flavours to honour the newlyweds.
